### 人 (rén)

- person, people
- [Pattern] Put <span style="color:red;">人</span> after a country/place to form the people from that country/place. For example:
    - 中国 :material-arrow-right: 中国<span style="color:red;">人</span> (China :material-arrow-right: Chin<span style="color:red;">ese</span>) :flag_cn: 
    - 北京 :material-arrow-right: 北京<span style="color:red;">人</span> (Beijing :material-arrow-right: Beijing-<span style="color:red;">er</span>)
    - 美国 :material-arrow-right: 美国<span style="color:red;">人</span> (America :material-arrow-right: Americ<span style="color:red;">an</span>)
    - 纽约 :material-arrow-right: 纽约<span style="color:red;">人</span> (New York :material-arrow-right: New York<span style="color:red;">er</span>)
