#### 这 (Zhè) & 那 (Nà)

- This, the pointed object is <span style="color:blue">closed to you</span>
- That, the pointed object is (far) <span style="color:red;">away from you</span>

#### 个 (gè/ge)
- 个 is a ^^measure word^^
